# GAIK - Generative AI-Enhanced Knowledge Management

Welcome to the **GAIK Project** documentation! This project develops advanced AI tools to solve critical business challenges in knowledge management and information access.

## Project Overview

The GAIK project leverages world-class generative AI (GenAI) to address organizations' critical business challenges in knowledge management and information access. We research and develop advanced AI tools that help companies eliminate information bottlenecks and significantly improve their ability to create, combine, and utilize essential business information.
